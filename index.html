Nursing home in Breclav

The competition project

While working on this competition project of the nursing home I took into consideration every simple comment of my clients.

The main idea of concept is to create a comfortable trail for the clients.

The trail includes 2 ways for a walk, first the small one for short walk and more comfortable for clients, who uses wheelchair. The second way is longer and covers the whole garden. Each way have a stops with intresting program and also with benches.

Neighbourhood Vinohrady

Vinohradska street (place next to the business college)

Sumavska street (place next to the school)

Luzicka street (place next to the restaurant)

Final thesis

The subject of bachelor thesis is Landscape studio of public place.

The thesis includes three parts.

The first part is theoretical which covers basic terms, the importance of the streets,described reference examples from abroad and the Czech Republic.

The second part of thesis is analytic part, deals with the chosen area, the neighbourhood Vinohrady. The part describes analyses, which one will be basic of design.

The design part is third part and includes author’s solution of model area, important part of design is a pedestrian traffic, streets be interwoven with city’s green area. Detailed design was done at the three streets in the neighbourhood Vinohrady.

  • Date: 2019
  • Category: Final thesis

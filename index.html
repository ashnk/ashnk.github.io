Gymnasium plan - design and execution.

A competition project

For this competition, I had to come up with a solution that comprehended the historical background of the courtyards.

The size of the courtyards was limited. I started with the first one. It's connected with the church of St. John the Baptist.

For this courtyard I proposed a medieval garden style. For the next courtyards I included a different style. The central cortyard serves as the stage, it has a fountain and it has space to stage a concert for the students. The last countyard is a beautiful open space with tree shadows, where students can spend free time.

  • Client: Gymnazium and SOS Mikulov
  • Date: 2018
  • Category: Plan of a school

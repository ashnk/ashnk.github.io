Nursing home in Breclav

A desighn contest

While I was working on this competition project of the nursing home, I took into consideration every single comment of my clients.

The main idea of the project is to create footpaths, which will be comfortable and attractive for everyone.

The designed trail includes 2 walks-ways: the first one is short and more comfortable option for all types clients who use a wheelchair.

The second walkway is longer and expands through the whole garden.

Both ways have a stops. with intresting activities.

  • Client: Nursing home,Breclav
  • Date: 2016
  • Category: Competition project

Gymnasium and SOS Mikulov

First courtyard

Second courtyard

Third courtyard

A Desighn Contest

For this competition, I had to come up with a solution that took into account the historical background of the courtyards.

The church St. Jallen is a part of the first yard and inspired the decor of this yard which reflects a monastery style.

The central courtyard is used as a stage for student's performances. The stage can be transformed into a fountain,where students can get refreshed during the hottest days in spring and summer.

The last courtyard is a beautiful open space with an abundance of shade created by the trees, where students can spend their free time.

  • Client: Gymnasium and SOS Mikulov
  • Date: 2018
  • Category: Competition project

Neighbourhood Vinohrady

Vinohradska street (place next to the business college)

Sumavska street (place next to the school)

Luzicka street (place next to the restaurant)

Final thesis

The subject of this bachelor thesis is a Landscape studio of public place.

The thesis includes three parts.

The first part is theoretical and covers basic terms, the importance of the streets,described reference examples from abroad and from the Czech Republic.

The second part of thesis is the analytic part, which deals with the chosen area and the neighbourhood Vinohrady. This part describes the analyses, one of which will be the basis of the design.

The design element is the third part and includes author’s description of the model area, such as that of pedestrian traffic which is an important design element, and streets being interwoven with the city’s green area. A detailed design was done at the three streets in the neighbourhood Vinohrady.

  • Date: 2019
  • Category: Final thesis
